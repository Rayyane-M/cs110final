Playlist Analysis

Popularity Distribution

  • X-axis represents all of the tracks in the playlist (limited to 100 songs due to the readability of the bar chart).
  • Y-axis represents the popularity of the songs:
    • Popularity score close to 100 indicates a song is extremely popular and popularity score close to 0 indicates low popularity of the song.
  • Purpose:
    • This chart provides insights into how well-known each track is.
    • It can help identify standout tracks that might resonate more with listeners or suggest tracks to explore further.

Track Length Distribution

  • X-axis represents all of the tracks in the playlist (limited to 100 songs due to the readability of the bar chart).
  • Y-axis represents the track length (in minutes)
  • Purpose
    • Useful for understanding the playlist leans towards short, punchy tracks or longer, immersive ones.

Genre Breakdown

  • Data Represented:
      Slices: Different genres represented in the playlist Slice Size: The number of tracks belonging to each genre (Represented as percentages)
  • Purpose:
      Provides an overview of the playlist's musical diversity. Helps indentify whether the playlist is dominated by one genre or includes a mix of various genres.
